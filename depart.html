DEPARTMENT ACTIVITIES

    • A student workshop is an educational event designed to provide students with practical knowledge and skills in a focused and interactive setting. It typically includes:
    Skill Development: Workshops aim to enhance specific skills or knowledge areas relevant to students' academic, personal, or professional growth. This could range from academic subjects like mathematics to soft skills like leadership or creativity.
    Hands-On Learning: Workshops emphasize active participation through activities, discussions, and exercises, ensuring that students apply what they learn in a practical way. This experiential learning approach helps students grasp concepts more effectively and prepares them for real-world challenges
